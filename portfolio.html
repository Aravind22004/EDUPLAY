General Subjects

In our curriculum, we cover a wide range of subjects to ensure a well-rounded education for students. The key subjects include Social Studies, where students gain an understanding of history,geography, civics, and current
affairs. In Telugu, we focus on enhancing language skills in reading, writing, and speaking. The Science curriculum explores fundamental concepts in physics, chemistry, and general science, while Mathematics develops problem-solving skills through arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and more. Biology covers the study of the living world, including plants, animals, and human biology. Our aim is to build a strong foundation in these subjects to prepare students for future academic challenges.

C Language

Our C Language course is meticulously designed to ensure students become proficient coders by the end of their schooling. The course is divided into two parts: in 9th class, students are introduced to C programming, covering basics like
syntax, data types, and control structures. In 10th class, they delve into advanced concepts, including functions, arrays, pointers, and file handling. Through rigorous practice and solving a variety of questions, we assure that students will become very skilled at coding.

Educational Games

To encourage and engage students in their studies, we provide interactive educational games after each class. These games are specifically tailored to each class and subject matter. For instance, after a video class, a game might present questions related to the topic covered, and students play
the game to find the correct answers. This method not only makes learning enjoyable but also aids in better retention of knowledge by reinforcing what they have learned in a fun and engaging way.

Live Classes and Score Tracking

Every Saturday, we conduct live classes where students can interact with teachers in real-time, ask questions, and receive immediate feedback. Additionally, all their scores from these classes and regular assessments are updated in their personal dashboard.
This system allows both students and parents to track progress and identify areas for improvement. By providing transparent and real-time updates on each student's performance, we build trust and ensure a supportive learning environment.
